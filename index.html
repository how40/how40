About

self explanatory

My name is Henry Wilson and I am a fan of creating things -- be it websites, videos, music, or art. Experienced in Java, Python, HTML, and CSS, I enjoy spending time on both ends of development. I find particular enjoyment on the UI/UX front -- as such, I'm majoring in Digital Narrative and Interactive Design at the University of Pittsburgh (with a CS minor).

In my free time, I am fascinated with obsolete technology -- turntables, cassette decks, miniDV camcorders, and old video game consoles. The way old technology can be used to both evoke nostalgia of old and tell new stories is an interest I chase through my multimedia project, dimwit .(note: some swearing and adult content)
